Hiring Process

Recruitment Workflow

Our clean, professional 5-step recruitment process to guide your application seamlessly from submission to onboarding.

Step 01

Apply Online

Submit your resume and cover details via our website or directly through email. Mention your specialized area in ELV or CCTV solutions.

Step 02

Resume Review

Our HR and engineering leadership teams evaluate your resume for core qualifications, certifications, and hands-on project experience.

Step 03

Technical Round

Participate in a deep-dive interview with our technical heads to discuss system architectures, practical troubleshooting, and site safety.

Step 04

Final Interview

A final HR round to align expectations, discuss role growth prospects, values, culture, and negotiate compensation package details.

Step 05

Welcome Aboard

Receive your formal offer, undergo orientation, get assigned your tools/SOPs, and begin working on high-impact security integrations.
