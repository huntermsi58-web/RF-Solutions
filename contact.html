Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our sales, installation process, and maintenance contracts.

Do you charge for site survey consultations?

No, we provide complimentary site surveys and consultations for corporate offices, residential communities, and industrial facilities across India. Our engineers will visit your property, assess entry points and security layouts, and provide an integrated solution blueprint.

What brands of security devices do you install?

We are authorized dealers and integrators for world-class brands including Hikvision, Dahua, Honeywell, Bosch, CP Plus, and Matrix. This ensures all cameras, biometrics, and alarm sensors we install carry official manufacturer warranties and reliable firmware support.

Do you support existing security setups?

Yes, we offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to optimize, monitor, and repair existing systems installed by other vendors. We perform routine system checks, cleaning, cabling replacement, and DVR/NVR storage integrity tests.

What is your response time for technical faults?

For corporate clients with active AMCs, we offer a 4-hour response time in major metro areas and a 24-hour turnaround for remote sites. Our phone support is active 24/7 to triage critical security breaches or server crashes.
